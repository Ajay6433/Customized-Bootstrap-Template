About Us

As computer ownership and internet connection speeds increasing across the globe e-learning becomes easily accessible. With that opportunities for more multimedia training methods arise called E-LEarning

How E-learning becomes the future of education here are some interesting facts about E-learning or you can say online education in India.

  • Many of the universities and colleges are conducting their course online and also certificated will be provided online.
  • It is estimated that there is a 175% increase in the cost of classroom education, this gives online education more preferred because it is cost effective.
  • "Learn from anywhere, at any time", "Saves Money and Time", "Learn at your own pace" and "Recognition of online degrees" are the major benefits of this.

The number of users enrolled for various online learning courses is estimated to be 1.6 Million in 2016, Which is expected to grow about 9.6 Million by the end of 2021.
